Hanan's Memorial Foundation

Towards the end of his bachelor's degree, he was supposed to start practical research work under the guidance of Dr. Ofra Zidon. However, the war altered his plans. Immediately after October 7th, Hanan was called to serve in the reserves. During the fighting in Gaza, he was seriously injured and subsequently got infected by a fungus that proved fatal after a struggle of several weeks.
